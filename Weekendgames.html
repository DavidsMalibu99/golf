Wolf — Round Setup

Wolf rotates automatically in tee order for holes 1-15 — each Wolf can't repeat a partner they've already picked. On holes 16-18, the Wolf is whoever is down the most money, with no partner restriction. Team score = best ball. Losing side pays the hole value into a pot, split evenly among winners. Stableford — Round Setup

Points: Eagle+=8, Birdie=4, Par=2, Bogey=1, Double Bogey=0, Triple Bogey or worse=-1. Your target per 9 is (36 - handicap) / 2, based on gross score (no per-hole strokes). Front 9 and back 9 are scored separately — whoever beats their own target by the most wins that 9, split evenly if tied. $20 buy-in splits $10/9 by default, but you can change the amount above.
